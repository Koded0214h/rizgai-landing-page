

> "Convince a business owner that this AI can answer calls, book appointments, qualify leads, and save money."

Every section should push that story.

# 1. Hero Section

This is where most visitors decide whether to stay.

### Contains

* Strong headline
* Short explanation
* CTA buttons
* Demo visual (phone call UI or dashboard)

### Example

**Headline**

> Never Miss Another Customer Call

**Subheadline**

> AI receptionists that answer calls 24/7, book appointments, qualify leads, and transfer urgent calls to your team.

**CTAs**

* Book Demo
* Listen to Sample Call

### Visual

Instead of stock images:

Show:

```
Incoming Call
↓
AI Receptionist Answers
↓
Books Appointment
↓
Sends Confirmation SMS
↓
Updates CRM
```

This immediately explains the product.

If you're rebuilding this from scratch in React/Tailwind, I'd aim for a premium feel similar to modern AI companies—clean spacing, subtle animations, dark/light gradients, interactive call-flow demos, and strong social proof rather than the generic SaaS-template look the current site has.
